This study uses the Malicious URL Dataset publicly available on Kaggle:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/marryjanety/phishing-url-dataset-url-and-label

The dataset consists of 651,191 URLs, each labeled with one of four categories: benign (legitimate, harmless URLs), phishing (URLs designed to impersonate legitimate services and steal user credentials or information), malware (URLs that host or distribute malicious software), and defacement (URLs belonging to websites that have been compromised and altered by an attacker, typically to display unauthorized content).
